Hiking the Highest Points in all 50 States

Andrew Nolan

1/17/2023

A common American bucket list item is to visit all 50 states. Some do this on road trips, seeing the biggest cities, rankining capitol buildings, or using rewards points to stay in hotels for free. I have a different goal, a much more difficult and probably unreasonable one, I want to climb to the highest point in each state.

This is not a short term goal. It’s something I expect to be working on opportunistically when I can for many years. I hope this document serves as accountability to my goal and a fun progress log as well.

All peaks are sorted in ascending height.
