The 12 Days of Cutesmas

By Andrew Nolan

Inspired by the famous English Christmas Carol

12 kitties drumming

11 cuties piping (these ones play music!)

10 cuties leaping

9 cuties dancing (actually it's only 2 but we can pretend)

8 milks of cuties

At least 7 swoobies swimming

6 cuties laying (on a bed or hammock)

5 GOLDEN CUTIES

4 calls to cutie

3 French cuties

2 cutie doves

A pair of cuties in a pear tree!

And a couple of cuties in a pear tree!
